Step-by-Step Guide: Compose a Message
Step 1: Compose a New Message 1. Click the "Compose" button in the top bar to start a new message. 2. In the recipient field, start typing the name or phone number of the contact you want to message. You can also select a contact from the dropdown suggestions. 3. Type your message in the message box below the recipient field. 4. If desired, you can attach media by clicking the attachment icon and selecting an image, video, or document from your device. 5. Click the "Send" button to deliver your message. Step 2: Group Messaging 1. To send a message to multiple contacts, enter multiple names or phone numbers in the recipient field, separated by commas. 2. Your message will be sent individually to each recipient, creating a group conversation. Step 3: Emoji and Emoticons 1. Express yourself with emojis and emoticons. Click the smiley face icon to access the emoji picker and choose from a wide range of expressive icons. Step 4: Formatting Text 1. You can format your text using basic styling options. Highlight the text you want to format and use the formatting icons to make it bold, italic, or underline. Step 5: Scheduled Messages 1. To schedule a message for later delivery, click the calendar icon before sending. Choose a date and time, and your message will be sent automatically at the scheduled time.
